About Me

I completed my Master's Degree at Indiana University and am continuing my studies with Professor Charlotte Hu at Boston Conservatory at Berklee.

What I Care About Right Now

Right now I care about deep listening, meditation practice, and growing creatively every day.

Meditation & Stillness

Meditation allows me to explore truth, life, and spirit through stillness. In quiet moments, I reflect on sound, silence, and the connection between music and awareness. Through practice, I seek clarity, balance, and deeper artistic expression both on and off the piano.
